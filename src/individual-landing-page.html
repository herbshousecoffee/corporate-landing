Meet The Herbster

A One-of-a-Kind Coffee Shop On Wheels

This is not a food truck. The Herbster is a custom-built, vintage-styled mobile coffee shop with a full espresso bar, cold brew dispensers, and professional barista crew. Obsessive attention to detail at every touchpoint.

  • Full espresso and beverage production station
  • Cold brew dispensers, hot and sparkling water systems
  • Warming drawers for fresh pastries and breakfast items
  • GPS tracking so you can watch it arrive in real time
  • Uniformed crew — professional, friendly, always smiling

Common Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

  • Herb's at the Curb is a membership-based premium coffee service. Instead of a coffee shop you go to, The Herbster — our custom-built vintage coffee truck — comes to you. We make every drink fresh at your door. No transport, no waiting, no compromises.
  • We are launching our pilot service in the Park Cities area and North Dallas (approximately a 2-3 mile radius). Future service areas will be determined by waitlist demand density — areas with concentrated interest get activated first.
  • Sign up for any membership tier and experience the full Herb's at the Curb service for 7 days, completely free. If it is not for you, cancel before the trial ends and you will not be charged. No risk, no hassle.
  • Baseline ($30/mo) gives you proximity and on-demand ordering with a 10% discount. Mid-Tier ($45/mo) adds one scheduled weekly delivery, a 15% discount, and a free branded tumbler. Household ($60/mo) includes two scheduled deliveries per week, up to 5 drinks per order, a 20% discount, and two free tumblers. All tiers include exclusive seasonal menu access.
  • Each morning, The Herbster stations at partner locations around the service area — schools, office buildings, community hubs. You order through the app and walk up or drive up to grab your freshly made drink. Think of it as a pop-up coffee shop in your neighborhood.
  • Yes. All memberships are month-to-month by default. Cancel anytime and your service continues through the end of your current billing cycle. Annual plans offer savings of approximately 15-20% (about 2 months free).
  • Delivery apps send someone to pick up your coffee and drive it across town. By the time it arrives, the ice has melted, the hot drinks have cooled, and the foam is gone. Herb's at the Curb is the opposite: The Herbster comes to your location and makes your drink right there, fresh, in front of you. It is a service experience, not a delivery.
